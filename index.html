About me

My name is Paulette Zaldivar Flores. I am originally from San Diego, California and currently based in Tokyo, Japan. I have been in education for over ten years. For the last eight years I have been teaching abroad mainly in Bangkok, Thailand and now Tokyo, Japan. Last year November 2021 I was introduced to coding by a friend who is a senior software enginner. Thanks to this introduction I enrolled in the SheCodes workshops which I am currently completing.

While completing the SheCodes workshops I have developed projects with advanced HTML, CSS, JavaScript, BootStrap, API and have learned to make responsive websites. I am also currently learning React.js. Front-end development is not only challenging but satisfying. The reward of completing a project that was coded on my own is far beyond any reward I have ever received. I am currently open to internships, junior part time and full time positions for front-end development. Feel free to contact me.
